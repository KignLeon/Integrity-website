Remove & Reinstall (R&R)

Replacing your roof? Don't let a roofer touch your investment. We safely detach, store, and reinstall your solar array.

Get a R&R Quote

The "Reroof" Hazard

Roofers are great at roofs, but they aren't electricians. Allowing untrained workers to remove your solar panels can void your warranty, damage delicate micro-inverters, and cause fire hazards upon re-connection.

We work directly with your roofing company to coordinate the timing. We show up, safely shut down and remove the system, store it (or stage it), and return once the new roof is ready to reinstall it to code.

Our R&R Protocol

1

Safety Shutdown

Complete de-energization of the DC/AC system.

2

Careful Removal

Mapping the array and safely lowering panels.

3

Re-Commissioning

Reinstalling with new flashings (waterproof) and verifying output.
