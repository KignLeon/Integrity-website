Level 2 Charging, Done Right

An EV charger draws as much power as an AC unit, but runs for hours at a time. Improper installation is the #1 cause of residential electrical fires in modern homes. We calculate your load, upgrade circuits if needed, and hardwire for maximum charging speed.

Universal Compatibility

Whether you drive a Tesla, Rivian, Ford Lightning, or Hyundai Ioniq, we install the correct NEMA 14-50 outlet or hardwired wall connector for your vehicle.

  • Fastest Charge Possible

    We size wire gauge to handle 48A or 60A charging, giving you ~40 miles of range per hour.

  • Permitted & Inspected

    Don't risk your home insurance. All our installs are permitted and code-compliant.

  • Load Management

    If your panel is full, we can install load management devices to allow EV charging without a heavy-up.
